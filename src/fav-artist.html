The Lumineers

About

The Lumineers are an American folk rock band formed in 2002. The band consists of Wesley Schultz, Jeremiah Fraites, and Lauren Jacobson. Their music is characterized by their use of acoustic instruments such as the guitar, mandolin, and piano, as well as their powerful harmonies and storytelling lyrics.
